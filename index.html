Tirukkuṟaḷ in English

The Tirukkuṟaḷ, or shortly the Kural, is a classic Tamil language text consisting of 1,330 short couplets, or kurals, of seven words each. Traditionally attributed to Saint Valluvar, it is considered one of the greatest works on ethics and morality and is known for its universality and secular nature.
